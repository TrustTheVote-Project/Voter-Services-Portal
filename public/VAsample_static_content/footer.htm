How to Register

Register online

To register to vote, you must complete a voter registration application. Use this web site to fill out the online application form.

You can also download a blank mail-in voter registration application and complete it by hand.

Other ways to register

Voters may complete registration applications in-person at a local election official office, Department of Motor Vehicles Customer Service Centers, and other Virginia government agencies. Learn more.
