How to Register

Register online

To register to vote, you must complete may use this web site to create online voter registration application and submit it to your local election official.

You can also download a blank mail-in voter registration application and complete it by hand.

Other ways to register

Voters may complete registration applications in person at a local election official office, DMV, and other Farallon government agencies. Learn more.
